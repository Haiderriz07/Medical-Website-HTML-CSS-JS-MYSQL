Our Main Units

Intensive Care Unit (ICU):

Our ICU provides 24/7 specialized care for critically ill patients. Equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by skilled intensivists and nurses, we offer precise attention during vulnerable moments, ensuring the best care for those in critical conditions.

Burn Unit

Our Burn Unit offers comprehensive care for patients with varying degrees of burns. With a team of burn care experts, including surgeons and wound specialists, along with advanced technologies, we're dedicated to restoring health and well-being for those on the path to recovery.

Radiology

Our Radiology Department combines advanced technology with experienced professionals to deliver accurate diagnoses. Using state-of-the-art imaging equipment, we capture detailed images for precise treatment planning, whether it's X-rays, CT scans, MRI scans, or ultrasounds.

Gynecology

The Gynecology Department provides comprehensive care for women's reproductive health. Our compassionate team offers services from routine check-ups to specialized care for complex gynecological conditions, prioritizing personalized well-being for every woman.

Nuclear Medicine

In our Nuclear Medicine Department, we utilize advanced imaging techniques with radioactive tracers to diagnose and treat a wide range of medical conditions. Our skilled team ensures precise diagnoses and tailored treatment plans for the highest standard of care.

Emergency Ward

Our Emergency Ward is a crucial lifeline for immediate and urgent medical needs. Staffed by dedicated emergency medicine professionals and equipped with advanced life-saving technology, we prioritize your well-being in times of crisis.
